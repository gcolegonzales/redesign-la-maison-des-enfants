Our Story

The same family feeling since 1970.

For more than fifty years, La Maison Des Enfants — French for “the house of children” — has been exactly that on Bayou Narcisse Road: a house full of children, laughter, and the kind of steady care that Gonzales parents have trusted across generations.

Some of the little ones who walked through our doors decades ago now bring their own children to us. That continuity is our whole philosophy. We keep classrooms warm, teachers familiar, and days predictable — because young children thrive on knowing exactly where they belong.

Programs by Age

A room that fits exactly who they are right now.

Children move up as they’re ready — from the nursery to the pre-K classroom to after-school. Each stage has its own space, rhythm, and teachers who know it well.

6 weeks – 12 months

Infants & Nursery

Our nursery runs on your baby’s schedule, not a clock on the wall. Feedings, naps, and cuddles follow the routine you know at home, written down and honored by caregivers who hold, rock, and talk to your little one all day long.

  • Individual feeding & nap schedules
  • Daily notes on bottles, diapers & naps
  • Tummy time, songs & gentle sensory play
1 – 2 years

Toddlers

Busy hands, big feelings. Toddlers here get room to move and explore safely, with gentle structure that turns everyday moments — snack, clean-up, story time — into first lessons in words, sharing, and confidence.

  • Early language & motor-skill play
  • Potty-training partnership with parents
  • Music, movement & messy art
3 – 4 years

Preschool & Pre-K

The kindergarten-ready years. Through circle time, letters and numbers, hands-on centers, and lots of play, our pre-K room builds the routines and school-readiness skills children carry straight into their first classroom.

  • Letters, numbers, colors & shapes
  • Circle time, learning centers & free play
  • Kindergarten-readiness & social skills
5 – 11 years

Before & After School

For our biggest kids, the day starts and ends with us. We provide transportation to and from school, a calm spot for homework, and time to just be a kid — so your morning and afternoon runs on schedule.

  • Transportation to & from school
  • Homework help & quiet reading time
  • Games, crafts & outdoor play

A Day in the Life

Predictable days, happy kids.

Little ones feel safest when they know what comes next. Here’s the gentle rhythm of a typical day — the exact times flex by age and by your child’s own routine.

  1. 6:00–8:30a

    Arrival & warm welcome

    Doors open at 6. Breakfast, quiet play, and a familiar face at the door for every child.

  2. 8:30–11:00a

    Learning & play

    Circle time, centers, art and stories — plus outdoor play when the Louisiana weather cooperates.

  3. 11:00a–12:30p

    Lunch & wind-down

    A calm sit-down lunch, hand-washing, books, and getting cozy for rest.

  4. 12:30–2:30p

    Rest & quiet time

    Naps for the little ones, quiet activities for those who’ve outgrown them.

  5. 2:30–6:00p

    Snack, school pickups & free play

    Afternoon snack, before/after-school kids arrive, and open play until pickup at close.
