# Drop real photos here

La Maison Des Enfants' only web presence is a Facebook page
(https://www.facebook.com/LaMaison1970/). Facebook images are token-blocked and
cannot be downloaded programmatically, so no real interior/classroom photos could
be sourced for this concept.

The site currently ships with tasteful, on-brand SVG/gradient placeholders. To make
it real, drop actual photos into this folder using the exact filenames below, and the
site will use them automatically (the CSS/markup already references these paths).

## Filenames the site looks for

- `hero.jpg`            — warm wide shot: children playing / bright classroom / front entrance
- `infants.jpg`        — infant/nursery room (cribs, caregiver holding baby)
- `toddlers.jpg`       — toddlers at a low table or in a play area
- `preschool.jpg`      — pre-K circle time / art / learning centers
- `schoolage.jpg`      — before/after-school kids doing homework or games
- `playground.jpg`     — outdoor playground
- `van.jpg`            — the transportation van/bus
- `director.jpg`       — director / staff portrait (Ellen Toups is listed as contact)

## Recommended specs
- Landscape, at least 1600px wide, JPG or WebP.
- Bright, natural light. Faces OK only with parent/guardian photo consent.

Until real photos are added, every placeholder is marked `<!-- IMG-NEEDED -->` in
index.html so nothing ships as if it were a real photograph.
